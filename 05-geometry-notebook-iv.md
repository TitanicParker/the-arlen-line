# [Editor’s Note: Right-Angle Lock Fragment]

This fragment follows the triple catalogue. It is the first place in the geometry notebook where a postponed value is allowed to enter.

The previous fragments repeatedly withheld the length of the longer boundary chords. They were visible. They belonged to a class. They pressed toward proof. But they could not yet be named by value. This page records the moment when that restraint is released.

## Geometry Notebook IV
### The Right Angle and the Waiting Length

The longer chord has been waiting.

AC, BD, CE, DF, EA, FB.

I have seen them. I have named their class. I have said they are longer than the neighbouring chords and shorter than the diameters.

But I have not been allowed to use their length.

This is important. The construction did not refuse the value because the value is absent. It refused the value because I had not yet earned it.

A fact can be near and still unavailable.

Now return to the boundary triangles that stand on diameters.

Choose one representative.

Let BE be the diameter.

Let F be a boundary point on the same circle.

Then triangle BFE has one side BE, one side FE, and one side BF.

Some things are already known.

BE is a diameter. It passes through O. It is made of two radii, so its length is two preserved openings.

FE is a neighbouring boundary chord. It carries the same preserved opening, so its length is one.

The missing side is BF.

But before BF can be found, the angle must be locked.

BE is a diameter.

F lies on the circle.

A point on the circle sees a diameter under a right angle.

Therefore the angle BFE is a right angle.

This must not be smuggled in from a square.

The right angle is not here because I have imagined a corner.

It is not here because the drawing looks right.

It is not here because the page secretly belongs to a grid.

It is here because the circle and the diameter have forced it.

The circle made the boundary.

The diameter crossed the boundary through the centre.

The boundary point F sees that diameter as a right angle.

Now triangle BFE may be treated as a right triangle.

BE is opposite the right angle, so BE is the hypotenuse.

BE = 2.

FE = 1.

BF is unknown.

The right-triangle relation can now be used:

BF^2 + FE^2 = BE^2

BF^2 + 1^2 = 2^2

BF^2 + 1 = 4

BF^2 = 3

BF = sqrt(3)

Now the waiting length may enter.

Not before.

Now BF is known as sqrt(3).

BF is one of the next-neighbour chords. By the same rotation, the value carries through the class:

AC,

BD,

CE,

DF,

EA,

FB.

Each has length sqrt(3).

This does not mean BF was magic. It means BF was the representative by which the class became lawful. The proof began in one triangle, but the sixfold structure carries it to the other members.

The value has travelled because the condition survives.

The pair catalogue can now be completed by length.

There are twelve joins of length one:

six radii,

six neighbouring chords.

There are six joins of length sqrt(3):

the next-neighbour chords.

There are three joins of length two:

the diameters.

The local length inventory is now:

1,

sqrt(3),

2.

These are not numbers floating above the figure.

One remembers the preserved opening.

Two remembers the diameter made from two radii.

sqrt(3) remembers the right triangle BFE, where a unit side and a diameter forced the missing side.

Every number must remember the act that earned it.

If a number forgets its act, it can still be correct and still become dangerous.

It can be used too early.

It can answer the wrong question.

It can make the page look finished when the route has not been walked.

This is why the waiting mattered.

The longer chord was not named when first seen. It was allowed to press forward. It was allowed to remain unfinished. Then the triple catalogue located the diameter-standing family. Then one representative was chosen. Then the right angle was earned. Then the right-triangle relation was allowed to operate. Then the missing length was locked. Then the class inherited the value.

That is the order.

Do not see and spend.

See, locate, wait, lock, then use.

Now return to triangle BFE.

Its sides are:

FE = 1,

BF = sqrt(3),

BE = 2.

The triangle carries the pattern:

1 : sqrt(3) : 2.

This is more than a list of lengths. It is the first stable comparison inside a right triangle. One side is half the hypotenuse. The middle side is sqrt(3) times the shortest side. The middle side is sqrt(3)/2 of the hypotenuse.

These comparisons are ratios.

A ratio is not another segment.

It does not add a new line to the page.

It says how one established length stands to another established length.

So the right angle has opened another kind of number.

Not count.

Not only measure.

Comparison.

But even here the names must wait.

I know that later someone may say sine, cosine, tangent.

Those names must not arrive first.

The comparison must exist before it can be named. The right triangle must be locked before the comparison exists. The diameter relation must be earned before the right triangle can be used. The boundary station must belong to the circle before the diameter can be seen rightly.

The order protects the meaning.

If the names arrive first, they may sound like knowledge while hiding the path.

The page teaches otherwise.

First the construction.

Then the right angle.

Then the missing side.

Then the pattern.

Then the comparison.

Then, only later, the names.

The boundary triangle catalogue can now be read again.

The 1,2,3 gap pattern gives the diameter-standing triangles. They now carry the 1 : sqrt(3) : 2 pattern.

The 2,2,2 pattern gives the alternating triangles. Their sides are next-neighbour chords, so they carry sqrt(3), sqrt(3), sqrt(3).

The 1,1,4 pattern gives the consecutive-station triangles. They carry 1, 1, sqrt(3).

The twenty boundary triangles now have a length ledger:

six carry 1, 1, sqrt(3),

twelve carry 1, sqrt(3), 2,

two carry sqrt(3), sqrt(3), sqrt(3).

The catalogue has changed because a proof has landed.

Nothing in the old catalogue was false. It was incomplete in a disciplined way. It knew what it could say and what it could only locate. Now the right-angle lock lets the earlier families be reread.

This is another kind of return.

A fact, once earned, becomes a lens.

It does not erase the earlier page.

It allows the earlier page to be read more exactly.

The longer chord was waiting.

The waiting was not emptiness.

It was fidelity to the route.

Now the route has reached it.
